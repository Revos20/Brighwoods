First-time home buyer's guide to Mukuru Affordable Housing

Published on Jan 20, 2026 by Brightwoods Editorial

The Mukuru Affordable Housing Project is a government-backed initiative designed to make homeownership a reality for everyday Kenyans. But how do you get started?

Understanding the Process

Buying your first home involves more than just the purchase price. In Mukuru, the focus is on community-centric living, meaning owners benefit from shared green spaces and modern infrastructure.

"Affordability doesn't mean compromising on quality. It means optimizing space for better living."

What to check:

Before committing, ensure you have your KRA PIN, national ID, and have registered with the Boma Yangu portal if applying for subsidized units.
